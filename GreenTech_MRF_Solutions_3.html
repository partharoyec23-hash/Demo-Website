What We Do

End-to-End MRF Project Services

From the first site survey to daily operational management — we handle every phase of your Material Recovery Facility project.

01
📋
Planning & Capacity Assessment
We conduct baseline surveys, waste characterisation studies, and future volume projections to correctly size your MRF for current and near-future requirements. Every project begins with data-driven planning.
02
🏗️
Construction & Commissioning
We prepare detailed design drawings and cost estimates, then manage civil construction and full machinery installation — conveyor belts, sorting platforms, baling units, and all ancillary infrastructure for complete operationalisation.
03
⚙️
Daily Operations Support
Once operational, we support dry waste segregation, mechanical sorting, and baling. Sorted recyclable fractions are dispatched to authorised recycling markets. Non-recyclable waste is stored for RDF processing or safe landfill disposal.
04
📊
Tracking & Monitoring
Systematic tracking of waste inflows, processing volumes, and outflows ensures accountability. We provide regular compliance reports to ULBs and relevant authorities — with digital data management for start/stop times and processing volumes.
